About Us ________________________________

The Plant Plate was started as a class project for the Web Technologies Module at Edinburgh Napier University in Scoland. The aim was the create
a singular website that people can go to whether they're just starting a more plant-based lifestyle, or are already plant pros!

Be honest, we all have the one recipe that we will never stop making. Passed down through generations? Found it when you were 15 and now can't stop?
No matter the reason, we believe that adopting a plant-based diet shouldn't mean not being able to make the recipes that you know and love.

Enter The Plant Plate! We have conveniently categorised some of the best homemade alternatives to whichever animal product you're trying to substitute, because going vegan doesn't have to be expensive.

Going plant-based can be confusing. We've just made it a little easier.

All recipes are made public without testing. Tried one of our recipes? Make sure to use the form to submit a review!
