Tofu Bulgolgi

Ingredients:

- 1 block of firm tofu, drained and pressed

- 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil

- 1/4 cup of soy sauce

- 2 tablespoons of brown sugar

- 2 tablespoons of mirin

- 1 tablespoon of rice vinegar

- 1 tablespoon of gochujang (Korean chili paste)

- 1 tablespoon of sesame oil

- 3 cloves of garlic, minced

- 1 teaspoon of grated ginger

- 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper

- 1/2 large onion, thinly sliced

- 2-3 cups of cooked bulgur or brown rice

- 1/4 cup of chopped scallions, for garnish

- Sesame seeds, for garnish

Instructions:

1. Cut the pressed tofu into thin slices or small cubes and set aside.

2. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, brown sugar, mirin, rice vinegar,
gochujang, sesame oil, minced garlic, grated ginger, and black pepper.

3. Add the sliced onions to the marinade and toss until they are evenly coated. Set
aside for about 10 minutes.

4. Heat up the vegetable oil in a large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat.

5. Add the marinated onions to the skillet and sauté for about 3-4 minutes, or until
they are slightly caramelized and softened.

6. Add the tofu to the skillet and sauté for about 5-6 minutes, or until they are
lightly browned and crispy.

7. Serve the vegan tofu bulgogi over cooked bulgur or brown rice, and garnish with
chopped scallions and sesame seeds.
