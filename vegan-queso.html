Vegan Cashew Queso

Ingredients:

- 1 cup raw cashews, soaked overnight or for at least 4 hours

- 3/4 cup unsweetened almond milk

- 1/4 cup nutritional yeast

- 2 tablespoons of cornstarch

- 2 tablespoons of tomato paste

- 1 tablespoon of lemon juice

- 1 teaspoon of chili powder

- 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder

- 1/2 teaspoon of onion powder

- 1/2 teaspoon of salt

- 1/4 teaspoon of smoked paprika

Instructions:

1. Drain and rinse the soaked cashews and place them in a high-speed blender or food processor.

2. Add the almond milk, nutritional yeast, cornstarch, tomato paste, lemon juice, chili powder,
garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and smoked paprika to the blender or food processor.

3. Blend the ingredients until the mixture is smooth and creamy. If it is too thick, you can add
a little more almond milk until you reach the desired consistency.

6. Transfer the cashew queso mixture to a saucepan and heat it over medium heat.

4. Stir the queso mixture constantly until it starts to thicken and becomes smooth, which should
take about 5-7 minutes.

5. Remove the pan from the heat and let the queso cool for a few minutes before serving.

7. Serve the vegan cashew queso with your favorite tortilla chips, tacos, or use it as a dip for
vegetables.
