Flaxseed "Egg"

Ingredients:

- 2 tablespoons of ground flaxseed

- 6 tablespoons of water

Instructions:

1. In a small bowl, mix together the ground flaxseed and water until well combined.

2. Let the mixture sit for about 5-10 minutes, or until it thickens and becomes
gel-like in consistency.

3. Use the flaxseed eggs immediately in your recipe as a substitute for eggs.

Note: 1 tablespoon of ground flaxseed mixed with 3 tablespoons of water can be used as a substitute for 1 egg
in most recipes. The mixture should be allowed to sit for 5-10 minutes to thicken before using in your recipe.
